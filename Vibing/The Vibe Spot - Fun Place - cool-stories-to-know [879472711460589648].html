I'm at it back again with something different than a webcomic this time! Today we have a CYOA, or choose your own adventure story. You've probably heard of these, and found the concept pretty neat. Well here is my all time favorite: Magium. A story about you, a regular human dude named Barry, joining a high stakes mage tournament where the end prize is something called the Magium. This Magium can supposedly give you magic, which you (Barry) have wanted for as long as you can remember. However, you're not entering this dangerous tournament completely unarmed. You've brought with you a stat device, which is a rare contraption that gives you the ability to invest points in abilities from anything to increasing your physical abilities, to understanding ancient languages, to even premonition. Armed with your stat booster and the friends you made along the way, you can discover lore about what this Magium actually is, unearth long forgotten secrets, survive long enough to make it to the end of the tournament, or die. Your choices throughout the story paired with what stats you invest into will determine what happens. The story has a lot of replay value as you can see a surprisingly different story with a few minor changes. Also there's secrets and achievements, which are really cool. Some notes: - Magium is an app available on Android and iOS - The first book is free, and it's a considerable amount of content - The second and third books cost a lil money, or you can unlock them by discovering a reasonable amount of achievements. - Magium is currently ongoing, updating every few months or so with deliciously long chapters - I love this book so much please read it
